FIFTY BUCKS SAYS I WIN EVERY TIME

GAMEPLAY

Roll all six dice.

Identify and “freeze” (set aside) one or more scoring dice (see Scoring below) and add the score to the player’s total for the turn.

If no scoring dice are rolled (a “zilch”), the turn is over, and the player scores 0 for that turn. The dice are then passed to the next player.

If desired, the player may continue rolling the remaining (unfrozen) dice in an attempt to increase their score for the turn. However, they must freeze some combination of scoring dice on each roll. If no scoring dice are rolled, it results in a zilch, and the player scores 0 for the turn.

If the player freezes all six dice, they have the option to “turn the corner” and roll all six dice again to continue adding to their score. If a zilch occurs after turning the corner, the player loses all points accrued during the turn, including those before and after the corner was turned.

If the player chooses not to roll again and has not zilched, they add the score for the turn to their total and pass the dice to the next player.
