FIFTY BUCKS SAYS I WIN EVERY TIME

GAMEPLAY

Roll all six dice.

Freeze one or more scoring dice and add them to your turn score.

If no scoring dice appear (a “zilch”), your turn ends with 0 points.

You may roll remaining unfrozen dice to try to score more, but you must freeze at least one scoring die each roll.

If all six dice are frozen, you may “turn the corner” and roll all six again. A zilch after cornering loses all turn points.

You can stop rolling at any time before a zilch to bank your points and pass the dice.

SCORING

Single 1s: 100 points each

Single 5s: 50 points each

Three of a kind: Number × 100 (except three 1s = 1000)

Four of a kind: Double the three-of-a-kind score

Five of a kind: Triple the three-of-a-kind score

Six of a kind: Quadruple the three-of-a-kind score

Straight (1–6): 1500 points

Three pairs: 1500 points

Two triplets: 2500 points

Extra 1s or 5s: Score individually even in combinations

Frozen dice: Only frozen scoring dice count

Zilch: No scoring dice ends your turn at 0 points

Cornering: Roll all six dice again if frozen, but risk losing turn points

End of turn: Bank points any time before a zilch

Passing dice: After ending, all dice go to the next player
